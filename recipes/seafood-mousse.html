The art of sea "Seafood Mousse"

Most normal restaurant in Ohio:

Super healthy fish dish that is even smiling back at you.

Seafood Mousse (aka Optimistic Fish) is a dish that will make you optimistic.

Ingredients:

  • Fish.
  • (Optional but needed) Chilli sauce
  • All purpose flour
  • Tomatoes
  • Salad
  • Oreo cookies

Instructions:

  1. Cover the fish in all purpose flour.
  2. Fry in high heat until the fish is cooked.
  3. On a plate, put salad and tomatoes.
  4. Put the fish on the salad.
  5. Put the Oreo cookies on the two eyes.
  6. Decorate and finish the fish with chilli saus (don't forget to make a smiley face).
